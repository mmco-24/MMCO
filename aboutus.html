Our Story

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional quality stainless steel solutions that exceed our clients' expectations through innovation, precision craftsmanship, and unparalleled service. We aim to transform spaces with elegant, durable steel products that stand the test of time.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of premium stainless steel products in Saudi Arabia, recognized for our dedication to excellence, innovative designs, and commitment to customer satisfaction. We envision expanding our influence across the region while maintaining our values of quality and integrity.

Eng. Omar Arman

CEO & Founder

With a pioneering vision and boundless passion, Eng. Omar Arman founded Modern Metals Trading Company, drawing on more than twenty years of deep experience in the stainless steel industry. With an unwavering commitment to delivering the highest level of craftsmanship and quality standards in every project, he has built a reputation as a trusted and distinguished destination for stainless steel solutions in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Eng. Omar Arman's vision is not only to meet customer expectations, but also to deliver innovative creations that combine technical precision and engineering excellence.

Our Journey

Founded in 2007, MMCO Trading Co. started its journey as a modest workshop with a focused specialty in custom stainless steel fabrication. Driven by a steadfast commitment to quality and an unwavering dedication to superior customer service, we rapidly ascended as a reputable and trusted name within the industry. Our early success was marked by our ability to deliver exceptional craftsmanship and adapt to the evolving demands of our clients, setting the foundation for substantial growth and expansion.

Today, we operate a state-of-the-art facility in Riyadh, equipped with the latest technology and staffed by highly skilled craftsmen who share our passion for excellence. Our portfolio spans residential, commercial, and institutional projects throughout Saudi Arabia.

In recent years, MMCO Trading Co. has expanded its services to include advanced design solutions and project management, catering to the unique needs of each client. Our commitment to innovation and efficiency enables us to undertake and deliver complex projects with precision and speed, ensuring client satisfaction at every step.

Looking ahead, MMCO Trading Co. is poised to set new industry standards by embracing sustainable practices and enhancing our technological capabilities. Our focus on sustainable development not only helps protect the environment but also offers our clients eco-friendly options without compromising on quality or aesthetics. We are excited to lead the way towards a greener future in stainless steel fabrication while maintaining the high standards our clients expect.
